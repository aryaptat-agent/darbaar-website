About

Darbaar is a record label rooted in house and techno, drawing from cultural and spiritual sonic traditions. Based in New York City, we exist at the intersection of modern electronic production and ancient rhythm.

The name means "gathering" — a space where sound, culture, and spiritual energy converge.
